Production & Inventory Management Solution with Zoho Inventory, Zoho Creator, and Zoho Books

Overview

Platform: Zoho Inventory, Zoho Creator, Zoho Books (integrated suite for manufacturing industry)

Key Features and Integrations

  • BOM (Bill of Materials) & MRP (Material Requirements Planning)
    • Comprehensive BOM module: Configure and maintain component lists for finished products. Visual BOM hierarchy provides complete material traceability and cost tracking.
    • MRP engine calculates material requirements based on sales orders, production plans, and historical demand, ensuring right-time procurement and minimum stock-outs.
  • Integrated Production Planning & Inventory Consumption
    • Create and manage work orders directly from sales or production schedules.
    • Automatic deduction of component inventory upon job closure, with real-time insight into raw material, WIP (work in progress), and finished goods inventory across multiple locations.
  • Inventory Synchronization & Adjustments
    • Sync inventory movements, PO generation, and financial transactions between Zoho Inventory and Zoho Books to ensure accurate, up-to-date accounting and inventory valuation without duplicate entry.
    • Support for inventory adjustments, transfers, and cycle counts with audit trails and reporting.
  • Quality Control
    • Quality checkpoints in the manufacturing process: Enforce inspection before completion, record defects or rework, attach photos/E-docs, and manage approval workflows for releases.
    • Customizable dashboards for tracking key quality metrics and compliance documentation.
  • Extensive Customization and Automation
    • Low-code automations for custom notifications, approval chains, and escalations (Zoho Creator).
    • Barcode/QR code scanning for swift material movements and order tracking.
    • Integrated with third-party logistics, e-commerce, and payment systems for unified operations.

Outcomes

  • End-to-end visibility of production, inventory, and financials across all business units.
  • Streamlined work order processing, material requirement forecasting, and procurement, minimizing downtime and wastage.
  • Fewer stock-outs and overstocking, thanks to real-time consumption and accurate MRP calculations.
  • Regulatory compliance and improved product quality via enforceable quality checkpoints.
  • Accelerated digital transformation for manufacturing—flexible, scalable, and fully integrated.
"Using Zoho Inventory and Zoho Creator together empowers manufacturers to control every aspect of production and inventory—BOM setups, MRP, warehouse, PO, work orders, and quality control—driving accuracy, efficiency, and growth."

In summary: This integrated manufacturing solution covers every stage from component sourcing to finished goods delivery. It automates procurement, empowers shop floor teams with accurate inventory, ensures quality, and unifies all data flows into finance and compliance modules for operational excellence and business growth.
